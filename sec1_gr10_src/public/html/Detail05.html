Leave your worries behind you and prepare to join us at PELUPO International

Music Festival at The Fields at Siam Country Club in Chonburi on March 11, 2023.

as activities to bring a proper festival experience to Thailand.

music lovers. For our inaugural festival, performances will be spread across two

stages in the midst of the green fields of Siam Country Club.
